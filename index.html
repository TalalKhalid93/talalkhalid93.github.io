About Me

I am a Ph.D. Candidate in the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering at the University of Illinois Urbana Champaign (UIUC), co-advised by Professor Kiruba Haran and Professor Ann-Perry Witmer. Through my research at UIUC, I seek to advance community-centric electric utility decision-making, using the theory of Contextual Engineering.
Before joining UIUC, I completed my Masters in Engineering and Engineering Management (major: Energy Planning and Policy) from the University of Technology, Sydney. Prior to that, I completed my Bachelors in Mechanical Engineering from NED University of Engineering and Technology.
